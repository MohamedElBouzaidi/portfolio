About meÀ propos

I'm a Senior Fullstack Java/Angular developer with 10 years of experience working across web projects and information systems in demanding sectors: banking, healthcare, and transport.

Specialized in backend development with Java 21 and Spring Boot, I care deeply about software quality — Clean Code, Design Patterns, and modern testing strategies (JUnit 5, Mockito, Testcontainers, Cucumber, ArchUnit) within microservices and hexagonal architectures.

On the frontend, I build Angular/TypeScript applications and have experience mentoring developers through code reviews and pair programming.
