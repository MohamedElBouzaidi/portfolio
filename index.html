About me À propos

I am a Fullstack Java/Angular developer with 10 years of experience working across web projects and information systems in demanding sectors: banking, healthcare, and transport.

Specialized in backend development with Java 21 and Spring Boot, I care deeply about software quality: Clean Code, Design Patterns, and modern testing strategies (JUnit 5, Mockito, Testcontainers, Cucumber, ArchUnit) within microservices and hexagonal architectures.

On the frontend, I build robust Angular/TypeScript applications. I lead teams, facilitate Scrum ceremonies, and mentor developers through code reviews and pair programming.

Je suis un développeur Fullstack Java/Angular avec 10 ans d'expérience sur des projets web et systèmes d'information dans des secteurs exigeants: banque, santé, transport.
